Profile


Dr. Rahul Singh is currently pursuing Super Specialty in Vascular and Endovascular Surgery at Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences, Hyderabad. Dr Singh believes there is a growing need for Vascular Surgeons in India, with recent innovations in the field of minimally invasive surgeries offering patients a breakthrough in Vascular Interventions.

Dr. Singh’s penchant to pursue medicine as a career was thanks to his dear mother, Dr. Rajani R. Singh’s contributions to the field of medicine. Dr. Singh chose General Surgery during his Master’s program and continued to work as a General Surgeon post-graduation. During this time as a General Surgeon he authored several publications and contributed to the field of general surgery.

He aspires to make a meaningful difference to the lives of patients and to drive innovation through patient centric treatment. Dr. Singh is willing to collaborate to understand the cutting edge technologies available, is excited to learn about the opportunities in the field of Vascular Surgery and endeavors to build lasting connections for a better tomorrow.
